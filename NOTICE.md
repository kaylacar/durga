## Distinguishing the projects

Durga and Khoj are not the same product.

- **Khoj** is the upstream project, including the hosted Khoj Cloud service operated by Khoj Inc.
- **Durga** is an independent fork. It does not offer a hosted service. There is no "Durga Cloud." There is no commercial relationship between Tech Enrichment and Khoj Inc.
